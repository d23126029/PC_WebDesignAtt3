Geology

At Tara Mines, geologists use advanced software, drilling techniques, and data analysis to identify ore deposits and guide excavation. They collaborate with engineers and surveyors to ensure efficient and sustainable mining operations. Join our team and contribute to innovation and discovery.

Surveying

At Tara Mines, surveyors use advanced tools like total stations, laser scanners, and drones to create detailed 3D models and ensure accurate excavation, control point setting, and subsidence monitoring. Their work optimizes efficiency, safety, and compliance, shaping the future of mining with precision and innovation. Join our team and be part of this cutting-edge work.
